About

This is a fan-made visualizer for the music of Deltarune. It's currently a work in progress.

Disclaimer

Personal interpretation is necessary for attempting to identify leitmotifs and connections between melodies. Please use your own judgement and compare resources before coming to conclusions.

FAQ

Q: Why did you make this?

For fun, mostly! I wanted a resource that compiled community knowledge in visual form, while trying to be as complete and accurate as possible. There is no analysis or in-depth descriptions here, just a visual representation of the connections between songs. Check out the Thanks section for useful links to resources that do provide more detailed analysis. More useful features coming soon.

Q: Will you add the Undertale songs too?

As of now, and for the foreseeable future, this project is focused only on Deltarune, and will include Undertale songs only when directly relevant.

Q: How do you organize the connections?

I try to stay consistent, prioritizing release order unless there's a thematic reason to do otherwise. Songs reference other songs through leitmotifs or otherwise, represented by edges.

Q: I noticed a mistake. / How can I contribute?

You can send me a message on Discord (@moxis) or Bluesky (coming soon). I'm happy to receive any comments or suggestions, or just to discuss any doubts. Thank you!
