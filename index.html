Welcome to Mox's Deltarune Music Visualizer!

How to use:
  1. Click on an arrow to highlight its leitmotif.
  2. You can drag songs to rearrange them.
  3. The layout is somewhat randomized, so if it looks messy you can try reloading.
  4. Certain "dark mode" extensions may affect colors.
X

About

This is a fan-made visualizer for the music of Deltarune. Songs are displayed and connected by arrows which signify leitmotifs or other melodies/patterns. It's currently a work in progress.

If you find a mistake or have suggestions, feel free to reach out on Discord (@moxis) or Bluesky (coming soon).

Disclaimers

This is a project to compile community knowledge, so many sources were referenced. Check out the Thanks section for links to resources that provide more detailed analysis.

Some choices in organizing or identifying leitmotifs can be subjective. This is my own attempt, so use your own judgement and compare resources before coming to conclusions.
