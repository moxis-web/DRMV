About

This is a fan-made visualizer for the music of Deltarune. It's currently a work in progress.

FAQ

Q: Why did you make this?

I wanted a resource that compiled community knowledge in one spot, while being editable, looking nice, and trying to be as complete and accurate as possible.

Q: How do you organize the connections?

I try to stay consistent, prioritizing release order unless there's a thematic reason to do otherwise. Songs reference other songs through leitmotifs or otherwise, represented by edges. The Freedom Motif having its own node is an exception.

Q: I noticed a mistake. / How can I contribute?

You can contribute by commenting on the GitHub or sending a message on Bluesky (coming soon). Thank you so much!
